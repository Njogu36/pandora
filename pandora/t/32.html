• At Hatch, we love seeing our customers’ awesome ideas turned into designs, ready for us to transform into beautiful printed materials. Our ethos is to create quality, ethical, professional print that can be shared, not only to generate real interest but also to impress and inspire others.

    We have a range of premium products available on our website and one of our most popular is the ‘Design your own’ Business Card. We have had an overwhelming response from all our amazing customers who have made some truly spectacular templates and it’s wonderful to see your photos of finished products shared across our social media channels!

    This has given us an idea; as we love nothing more than seeing and sharing our spectacularly talented customers’ designs, we have thought of a way to do this whilst bringing London together through imaginative, print design. Presenting London’s newest design competition #LondonCreative.

    We are Print. We are London. We are Hatch.

    How to enter #LondonCreative

    It’s simple; enter our competition by uploading your file using the form below or send us an email with your design to [email protected].

    To be in with a chance to win, be sure to also Like us on Facebook, and Follow us on Twitter and Instagram to make your entry valid. Details of your Facebook name, Twitter and Instagram handle must be included if you are entering via email. You can share a snap of your design using the hashtag #LondonCreative (we would sure love to see all your entries plastered across our social channels!).

    The competition will run from 2nd – 31st October and the winner will be selected and announced on Friday 3rd November 2017 via Facebook, Twitter, and Instagram.

    Prizes

    One lucky winner will; have their design made available as a template on Hatch, with their name and Twitter handle displayed on our website, have their winning business cards printed for free and receive a Wacom Pad*!

    We look forward to seeing all your entries and Good Luck!
